## Problem

Predict is person healthy or tumor using neural nets by gender and certain genes.
Certain genes are SEMA7A, GATA4, LHX2, SOST, CTLA4, NMNAT2, ZFP42, NPAS2, MYLK3, NUDT13, KIRREL3, FKBP6, SOST, NFATC1,  TLE4

Input: gender and certain genes
Algorithm: neural nets
Output: healthy or tumor

## Data Structure:

Each row is patient data.

Columns:
1. Avg Methylation % of a certain gen.
2. Gender
3. Output - is patient healthy or not

Example:
Patient_id | SEMA7A | GATA4 | ...     |  Gender | Output

   TCGA1      average composite value     Male      1
